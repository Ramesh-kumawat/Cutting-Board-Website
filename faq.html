Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What materials are your cutting boards made from?
A: Our cutting boards are made from high-quality, sustainably sourced hardwoods, such as maple, walnut, and cherry.
Q: How should I care for my cutting board?
A: To ensure longevity, hand wash your cutting board with warm soapy water and dry it thoroughly. Avoid soaking or putting it in the dishwasher. Regularly oil your board with food-grade mineral oil to maintain its condition.
