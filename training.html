Training Programs

At Cutting Boards, we offer comprehensive training programs to help you get the most out of our products and enhance your culinary skills. Our programs are designed for both beginners and experienced chefs.

Basic Cutting Techniques

Learn the fundamental cutting techniques that every chef should know. This training covers everything from proper knife handling to basic cutting styles like julienne, dice, and chop.

Advanced Knife Skills

Take your knife skills to the next level with our advanced training. Perfect for professional chefs and culinary enthusiasts, this program covers precision cuts, carving, and advanced knife maintenance.

Cutting Board Maintenance

Learn how to properly care for your cutting boards to ensure they last for years. This program includes cleaning techniques, oiling, and tips for preventing cross-contamination.
